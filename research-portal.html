廣告創意翻譯是什麼？What is advertising transcreation?
直譯 Direct Translation
創意翻譯 Transcreation
機器翻譯
"This product has the function of making your face become white and also smooth, recommend to use every day morning and evening two times."
（中文原意：本產品可使臉部變白光滑，建議每日早晚各使用一次）
AdWhiz 創意翻譯
"Wake up to radiant, porcelain-smooth skin. Formulated for twice-daily use — because your glow doesn't take days off."
（中文大意：每天早晚使用，喚醒光滑透亮肌膚——你的光采不需要休息）
再看一個例子 — 珍珠奶茶品牌
直譯
"Our bubble tea uses the best ingredients from Taiwan, the taste is very unique and special, welcome to come and experience."
（本店珍珠奶茶使用台灣最優質食材，口感獨特，歡迎蒞臨體驗）
創意翻譯
"Taiwan's finest — chewy pearls, rich milk tea, zero compromises. Come taste the obsession."
（台灣最好的——Q彈珍珠、濃郁奶茶，毫不妥協。來嚐一口就上癮的滋味）
